Child / Kids

Good oral practices start with birth of the child. Pediatric dentist is especially trained to handle dental problems of a kid.

Did you know your child’s first dental visit should be at the age of 6 months? Need not worry, its never too late. Book your child first dental online consultation now!

Men

Good oral health has been linked with longevity studies and surveys have shown that men are less likely than women to seek preventive dental care and often neglect their oral health for years, visiting dentist only when problem arises.

When it comes to your oral health, the earlier the better.

Women

Women have special oral health requirements during the unique phases in their lives.

Due to the hormonal changes occurring throughout a women’s life, females are more prone to gum diseases and eating disorders.

Know more about the change to oral health and importance of professional and home care.

Senior Citizen

Chronic illness in older adults such as diabetes, or heart diseases put seniors at high risk for dental problems, like toothache, gum diseases and tooth loss.

Book a consult and understand why it’s important to make dental health for seniors a priority and how to help your elderly loved ones improve their well-being.
